Preparing young people with SEND for adult life

Information for young people with special educational needs and disabilities (SEND) about the time before and after you turn 18.

Before you are 18 years old, various council services can help you. When you reach 18, you will become an adult and that support will end.

Find out how your support will change up to the age of 18 and how you can take more control of your life. This time is often called transition.
